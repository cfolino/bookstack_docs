# pi hole primary

* Primary DNS resolver with ad-blocking and local DNS capabilities for the homelab.
* Role: Local DNS
* Platform: Pi-hole
* Hostname: pihole.cfolino.com
* Management URL: pihole.cfolino.com/admin
* Management IP: 192.168.10.44
* VLAN: 1
* Recursive DNS resolution through Unbound.
* Configuration: /etc/unbound/unbound.conf.d/
