# pi hole secondary

* Secondary DNS resolver providing redundancy for local name resolution and DNS filtering.
* Role: DNS Backup
* Platform: Pi-hole
* Hostname: pihole-backup.cfolino.com
* Management URL: pihole-backup.cfolino.com/admin
* Management IP: 192.168.10.46
* VLAN: 1
* Recursive DNS resolution through Unbound.
* Configuration: /etc/unbound/unbound.conf.d/
